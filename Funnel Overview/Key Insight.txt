Funnel Overview:
Low product view rate suggests that users are not engaging beyond landing pages, indicating potential issues with homepage design, unclear value proposition, or ineffective traffic targeting.
Only ~20% of product viewers add items to cart, suggesting gaps in product attractiveness, pricing perception, or insufficient product information.
Relatively strong cart-to-checkout conversion (77%) indicates low friction in early purchase intent stages, but a 45% checkout completion rate suggests room for optimization in payment experience or trust signals.

Problem 1: Low product views
Recommendations:
Improve homepage UX (clear product categories)
Personalize recommendations
Optimize traffic targeting (ads mismatch)

Problem 2: Low add-to-cart
Recommendations:
Improve product pages (images, reviews, descriptions)
Highlight pricing/discounts
Add urgency signals (limited stock)

Problem 3: Checkout drop
Recommendations:
Simplify checkout flow
Enable guest checkout
Reduce hidden fees
